Common trip scenarios

These are real‑life cases where an app like this saves fights and confusion.

  • Friends’ trip: Different people pay for fuel, stay, food. A splitter makes sure all those payments end in one clean “who pays whom” list.
  • Roommates: Monthly rent, electricity, Wi‑Fi, and cleaning services split fairly even if one person pays the landlord every time.
  • Office team outings: One person pays the restaurant, one pays for cabs, and later everyone settles without spreadsheets.
